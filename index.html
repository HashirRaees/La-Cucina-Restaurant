Our Story

Nonna's recipes, reimagined for today

La Cucina opened its doors in 1987 when Chef Marco Conti brought his grandmother's handwritten recipe book to a 12-seat trattoria in North Beach. Three decades later, that same soul guides every dish we serve.

"Cooking is a conversation between the ingredients, the season, and the person you're feeding."

— Marco Conti, Executive Chef & Founder
  • ✓ Every pasta made fresh in-house, every morning
  • ✓ Ingredients sourced from local farms and our kitchen garden
  • ✓ Private dining room for up to 30 guests
  • ✓ Seasonal menu updated quarterly by the chef
